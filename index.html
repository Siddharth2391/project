Welcome to Charity Funding

Blockchain and smart contracts create new prospects for charity by converting aid into digital assets and increasing confidence in nonprofit organizations. Donors may now track the effect of their gifts, and organizations can utilize crypto tokens to motivate donation.

What is blockchain?

Blockchain defined: Blockchain is a shared, immutable ledger that facilitates the process of recording transactions and tracking assets in a business network. An asset can be tangible (a house, car, cash, land) or intangible (intellectual property, patents, copyrights, branding).
